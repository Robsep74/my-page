About Me

Luis Roberto Sepulveda provides Web Designing Services in the San Antonio, Texas area.

He is currently specializing in Web Design through the UTSA San Antonio online course offered by Webucator.

Mr. Sepulveda goes by his middle name, Roberto, or Robert. He moved to San Antonio, Texas, in 1999 to work in the natural stone industry, where he got involved in different departments within the company and developed a wealth of knowledge from customer service, sales, project management, production, installation, and quality control.

Roberto was also involved in the marketing department, creating brochures and promoting the company's products. He is familiar with computer programs such as Power Point, GIMP, and Photoshop, as well as other office-related softwares like Excel and AutoCad. These days, he is focus on pursuing his passion for web design and web development, and he will be ready to offer his services soon.
