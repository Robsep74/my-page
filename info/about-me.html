"Better Call Robert"

Robert Sepulveda provides Web Designing Services in the San Antonio, Texas area.

He is currently specializing in Web Design through the UTSA San Antonio online course offered by Webucator.

Mr. Sepulveda moved to San Antonio, Texas, in 1999 to work in the natural stone industry, where he got involved in different departments within the company and developed a wealth of knowledge from customer service, sales, project management, production, installation, and quality control.

Robert was also involved in the marketing department, creating brochures and promoting the company's products. He is familiar with computer programs such as Power Point, GIMP, and Photoshop, as well as other office-related softwares like Excel and AutoCad.

After deciding to pursue one of his lifelong passions in the digital world, Robert left the natural stone industry and enrolled in courses through Webucator to deepen his skills. What began as a hobby in digital design evolved into a focused interest in web design — and ultimately, a new passion: web development. Today, he offers his services to established businesses and small startups alike, whether they need a brand-new website, a refresh of their current one, or support with digital advertising and design.

In the digital space, Robert has experience producing a wide range of video content using editing software such as Filmora, iMovie, and Final Cut Pro. He offers professional video editing services and can create custom content tailored to your business needs. For advertising, he designs digital ads using tools like Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Keynote, and GIMP — and is now expanding into AI-powered creative workflows. He also composes background music and provides voice-over narration in both Spanish and English. Robert is well-versed in social media platforms and understands how to craft videos and ads optimized for online audiences.
